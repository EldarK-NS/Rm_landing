About the Project

Recruit Master is a mobile candidate marketplace with an integrated CRM/ATS recruitment management system. It brings recruiters and employers together on a single platform, enabling quick access to pre-screened, high-quality candidates and ensuring a transparent hiring process with ratings, comments, and full candidate history.

For employers, this means faster hiring without spending weeks on sourcing. For recruitment agencies, it’s a way to monetize candidate databases, manage the entire recruitment process from a phone, and collaborate with clients in real time.
